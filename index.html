Click to join!

Feel like working out is a chore? Tired of lacking motivation to do a workout? Then Fitchallenge may be just for you. On fitchallenge you can use our challenges as motivation to do your workouts. The challenges you complete can be compared with your friends so you can see which one of you has the most. We also offer a large list off example exercises so you can do them in correct form, along with the help from our meal plan you will reach your goals in no time!


Your own profile

Make your own profile so you can register your exercises. Share your trophies with friends, and compete to see who has the most and best trophies.



Challenges

The site offers a wide variety of challenges to unlock, use these as your motivation. Log your workouts and progress towards unlocking bigger and better challenges!



Meal plan

With the “meal plan” you can get tips on how to eat correct according to what kind of goals you are working towards. The plan will be updated every week so you will get new and exciting meals while still eating right.



Exercises

We offer a large variety of examples on how to perform your workout properly. This way you will get the most out of each exercise while not ruining your body in the progress!
